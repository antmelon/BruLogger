# BruLogger

A personal app for logging and tracking your coffee brews.

## What it does

Log each brew with your parameters and tasting notes, rate the result, and build up a history over time. The analytics tab shows how your ratings trend, which methods you reach for most, and your average flavor profile across all brews.

## Using the app

Sign in with Google, then hit "Log Brew" to record your first brew. Each entry supports brew parameters (dose, yield, grind size, water temp, brew time), a flavor profile, tasting notes, a star rating, and an optional photo.

Your brew history is private — only you can see your logs.
